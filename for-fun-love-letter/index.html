My love letter to my future wife

I can't wait to start my life with you

Dear Beloved MDN, how do you do?

I hope this letter finds you in the best of health alongside your beloved Mozilla family. Forgive me for taking too long of a time to write you this letter, but I was quite busy with matters pertaining to my family. My parents held a housewarming event at our new house and it was wonderful albeit exhausting, but I digress.

I hope our time away has not make you forget me. As they say,

Absence makes the heart grow fonder.

Here's my humble attempt at doing so:

Our time was brief but magical. And everyday hurts without you by my side.

If I could, I would (and I should) recreate the potrait that I painted of you during our time together.

The quality's fantastic if I say so myself. Of course, your beauty enabled this rendition of mine to happen. I am merely a conduit of your Aphroditic magnificence to the world.

Everyday I prayed to the Almighty for His Blessings and He showered me with His Encompassing Love: in the form of you.

For the time being, this portrait will do until we meet again.

My friends say that I am brilliant.

But nothing could be further than the truth for I am merely the face and you are the brain.

I still remember when you taught me about the elements. My favourite one was and is always you. But if a preposterous hypothetical would be imposed upon myself to pick another one, I would not dare to think of it.

But there is something to be said about <kbd></kbd>. When the browser renders Ctrl + Shift + R, I feel myself losing control.

Forgive me, my love. I've letten go of my emotions there for a bit. Excuse me while I commit the Japanese seppuku for my betrayal.

My devotion is entirely yours.

Before I go...

Please keep this photograph as a memento of our time together. You look beautiful.

Forever yours,

Irfan Zainudin
